Assignment 2.2

How do we know whether the following sentences are
syntactically correct or incorrect?
– The dog loves the cat
– A rat lives in the house
– I kicks dog that
– Love you I do
– We school go to

----------------------------------
The dog loves the cat is correct
A rat lives in the house is correct 

I kicks dog that is not correct
Love you I do is not correct
We school  go to is not correct
